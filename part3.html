07 — Graphic Language

Holding Shapes

Rounded containers that crop imagery or 3D scenes — always portrait or landscape, never a perfect circle or square. Radius equals 50% of the shorter dimension. Aligned to 90° only.

CSS — Holding Shapes

  





  
Construction
Round corners until short edges become perfect semi-circles. No flat segments on the short sides.

  
Ratio
Length-to-width 1.4:1 to 2.5:1. Avoid ratios beyond 3:1 (too narrow) or 1:1 (circle).

  
Montage
Multiple images in one holding shape. Ensure visible gap and strong contrast with background.

  
Avoid
Perfect circles, too-narrow shapes, non-90° angles, custom organic outlines.

09 — Motion & Animation


Motion Principles


Azure motion expresses Forward and Infinite principles — purposeful direction, data flows, and orbital scale. All animations below are live CSS. Copy any keyframe block for use in your projects.



  
Data Flow
Particles moving left-to-right — Forward principle. Use for data streaming, pipeline, AI processing moments.

  
Orbital Scale
Pulsing rings with orbiting element — Infinite principle. Global infrastructure, vast computing scale.

  
Rising Data
Bar elements rising from baseline — growth, forward momentum. Use for metrics and dashboards.

  
Path Flow (SVG)
Dots animate along a Bézier curve — ideal for data pipeline diagrams, service routing, AI inference flows.

  
Breathing Holding Shapes
Holding shapes softly pulsing — subtle life on static layouts. Use sparingly on hero sections.

  
Scan Lines
Horizontal light sweeps — Digital / Hologram texture animation. Use as loading state or background motion.
