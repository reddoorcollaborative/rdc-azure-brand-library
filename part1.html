02 — Design Principles


Build in Public


Six principles govern every Azure creative decision. Each card below is designed using the principle it describes — the layout IS the demonstration.



  
01
Immersion
Dive into
captivating detail
Rich activity and detail, in both physical and conceptual spaces. Intimate, cropped views that pull the viewer in as a collaborator.

  
02
Infinite
Vast scale,
limitless possibility
Limitless innovation through scale — 3D unlimited data flows, cinematic wide views, compositions that feel boundless.

  
03
Expert
Technical precision,
schematic clarity
Deep expertise through schematic wireframe illustrations, precise headlines, and clean architectural lines.

  
04
Curious
Beautiful in
the specifics
Close-up and macro photography. Microfluidic chips, circuit details, fiber optic nodes. The frontier is gorgeous up close.

  
05
Forward
Momentum,
direction, motion
Forward momentum through central focal points, moving subjects, and directional 3D flows. Things always moving rightward.

  
06
Authentic
Real people,
real places
Real people and places. Lived-in wardrobes, personal effects on desks, natural expressions. Uniquely Azure.









03 — Logo


Logo Usage


The positive (full-color) logo is primary. For dark or color backgrounds use the reverse or single-color variants. A stacked secondary logo is available for tight spaces.



  
Positive Logo · Light Background · Primary

  
Reverse Logo · Dark Background · Primary

  
Single-color · Color Bg Medium Value

  
Full-color Logo · Dark Color Bg




Clearspace
Minimum clearspace of 1× the logo symbol size on all sides.
Layout Margins
Minimum margin of 1× or 2× the logo symbol in all layouts.
Product Icon ≠ Logo
The Azure product icon appears in dashboards only. Never use it in marketing materials.
